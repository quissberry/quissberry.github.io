Animals

Animals is a series of music tracks, titled after different animals. Most of the animals I have chosen are arbitrary, sometimes making sense, sometimes without makign any sense. However, my intent with these tracks is for the audience to make their own interpretations of how the animal connects to the music.

A bee: homebrew scale + beats


An elephant: march + 7th chords


A giraffe: pushing melodies (2023年7月21日)


A lemming: inspired by traditional khaen music


A tapeworm: simple waltz with two melodies
