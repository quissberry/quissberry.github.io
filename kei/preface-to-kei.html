Preface to Kei

Published on 2024年6月30日

Kei is a quarterly series where I draft a new chapter every three months; one for summer, autumn, winter, and spring. After revising, I will periodically post them by chunk. Although the chapters are very disjointed, the series centers around the character Kei and the greater Kei universe. The series will be oriented towards sci-fi, fantasy, and thriller primarily, though the main purpose of the series is to write whatever ideas I have, free of genre.

My goal with this project is to get more practice in writing and build up a better writing habit. As a person who never finishes anything, I also just want to get stories released, hence the reasoning for this format.
